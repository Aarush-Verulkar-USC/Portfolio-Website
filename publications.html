My research work focuses on innovative applications of blockchain technology in healthcare and developing secure, efficient systems for electronic health records.

Intellihealth: Intelligent Health Record System

IEEE ICBDS October 2024

This paper presents a blockchain-based decentralized Electronic Health Record (EHR) system with role-based access control. The system integrates a patient-assisting chatbot and machine learning models for preliminary disease detection from medical imaging, achieving over 95% accuracy. Our approach addresses key challenges in healthcare data management including security, interoperability, and accessibility while maintaining patient privacy.
